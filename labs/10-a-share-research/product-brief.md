# Product brief: A-share research and strategy sandbox

## Goal

Help a learner study one A-share company with consistent evidence and compare
a simple strategy against buy-and-hold using fixed historical samples.

## User

A learner who can use a browser but does not need programming or quantitative
finance experience.

## First-version boundary

- One sample company: 示例科技（`600001`，虚构教学标的）.
- Fixed daily prices, one financial snapshot, and two announcement cards.
- One moving-average crossover strategy.
- Research and backtesting only.

## Done when

The learner can open the web page, inspect source cards, run a backtest, and
explain at least two limitations without seeing a trade recommendation.

## Prohibited

No brokerage connection, real order, live quote dependency, investment advice,
or return promise.

## 中文摘要

- **目标**：用一致流程整理公司资料、区分事实与解释，并在固定历史样本上
  验证简单双均线策略。
- **用户**：会使用浏览器、但不具备编程或量化背景的学习者。
- **完成标准**：能阅读来源卡、运行回测、解释至少两条限制；页面不得出现
  买卖建议。
- **禁止**：证券账户、真实下单、实时行情依赖、荐股、收益承诺。
