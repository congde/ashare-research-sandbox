# Ticket: prepare the entry for the note-app research

Prepare an execution environment for the research task in
`labs/04-research/ticket.md`.

## Required outcome

Create an entry decision that:

- identifies the minimum capabilities needed by the task;
- chooses an entry based on those capabilities rather than personal habit;
- records a concrete probe and result for each required capability;
- defines a stop or downgrade action for every failed or unavailable probe;
- defines the workspace rules that the executor must read before starting;
- leaves pricing verification and the final migration decision to a human.

## Done when

- `verify.py` passes against the entry decision;
- every required capability has a probe result and evidence location;
- a human confirms that no `passed` claim is unsupported.
