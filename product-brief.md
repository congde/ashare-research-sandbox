# Product brief: Web3 research and strategy sandbox

## Goal

Help a learner inspect one fictional Web3 asset with traceable evidence and
compare a simple strategy against buy-and-hold using fixed offline samples.

## User

A learner who can use a browser but does not need programming, quantitative
finance, exchange accounts, or API keys.

## First-version boundary

- One fictional teaching asset: `示例协议（WEB3-DEMO/USDT）`.
- Fixed daily prices, one market/activity snapshot, and three source cards.
- One moving-average crossover strategy.
- Research and backtesting only.

## Done when

The learner can inspect source cards, run a backtest, and explain at least two
limitations without seeing a trade recommendation.

## Prohibited

No exchange connection, wallet signing, real order, live quote dependency,
investment advice, or return promise.

## 中文摘要

- **目标**：用固定 Web3 数据包区分事实与解释，并可重复验证双均线策略。
- **用户**：会使用浏览器，但不要求编程、量化或交易所账户经验的学习者。
- **完成标准**：能阅读来源卡、运行回测并解释至少两条限制。
- **禁止**：交易所账户、钱包签名、真实下单、实时行情、投资建议与收益承诺。
